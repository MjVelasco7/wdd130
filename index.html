Los Bancos

San Miguel de los Bancos is a place where you can find a warm and humid climate. Furthermore, it is very close to the capital of Ecuador. Los Bancos belongs to Pichincha, which is the capital of Ecuador. Also, the place is surrounded by forests, birds, butterflies, and a different variety of animals and plants. It is to say, there are several varieties of flora and fauna.
