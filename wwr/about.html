Los Bancos Rafting

Our commitment: Providing thrilling rafting adventures, championing eco-consciousness, and crafting unforgettable moments. Driven by safety, expertise, and zeal. Advocating for the conservation of local biodiversity, we aspire to ignite a love for nature and exploration.

History

Established in 1995, Los Bancos Rafting, directed by Maria Velasco, provides exhilarating river escapades amidst Otavalo's scenic beauty. Evolving from modest roots, we've excelled in safety, expertise, and creating remarkable journeys. Deeply rooted in forest conservation, we're devoted to nurturing our natural surroundings.
